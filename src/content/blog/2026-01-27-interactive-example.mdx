## On-Page SEO Checklist

Here's what every page needs:

- ✅ Title tag (50-60 characters)
- ✅ Meta description (150-160 characters)
- ✅ H1 tag with primary keyword
- ✅ H2/H3 structure
- ✅ Internal links
- ✅ External links to authority sites
- ✅ Images with alt text
- ✅ Mobile responsive
- ✅ Fast loading speed

Technical SEO is the foundation. Here are the must-haves:

1. **HTTPS** - Security is a ranking factor
2. **Mobile-first** - Google indexes mobile version first
3. **Page Speed** - Aim for under 3 seconds
4. **XML Sitemap** - Help Google find all your pages
5. **Robots.txt** - Control what gets crawled
